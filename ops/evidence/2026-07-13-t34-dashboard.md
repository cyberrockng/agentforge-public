# T3.4 Founder dashboard

Date: 2026-07-13

## Scope implemented

- Replaced the placeholder dashboard with a ledger-backed dashboard at `/dashboard`.
- Dashboard reads from a committed T3.2 ledger snapshot, not sample data.
- Current visible totals:
  - paid calls: `1`,
  - settled: `1.000000 USDT`,
  - Forge revenue: `1.000000 USDT`,
  - founder payable: `0.000000 USDT`.
- Tenant rows:
  - AgentForge: one paid `/svc/forge` call from the First Heartbeat backfill,
  - ShieldCheck: zero paid calls; gated, own heartbeat pending,
  - Launch Kit: zero paid calls.
- Latest-call panel shows the real ServiceCall ID, ledger transaction ID, tenant, service, amount, delivery timestamp, and payment transaction.

## Reality boundary

- No fake revenue, founders, customers, receipts, or reviews were added.
- ShieldCheck remains zero-paid-call because the known 1 USDT tx paid `/svc/forge`, not `/svc/shieldcheck`.
- The dashboard is a committed ledger snapshot. Future production paid calls must be added through the T3.2 journal/persistent ledger path before they appear as dashboard numbers.

## Verification

Dashboard summary tests prove:

- only the committed First Heartbeat backfill contributes revenue,
- ShieldCheck stays at zero until its own paid call exists,
- atomic USDT formatting does not round.
